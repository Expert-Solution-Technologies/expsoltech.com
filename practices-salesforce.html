The customizability of Salesforce CRM is extremely high; hence we provide you the best results every time. As an offshore Salesforce Development Company, we at Expert Solution Technologies have years of expertise to meet the varying customer needs covering a number of fields in the industry.

Our proficient team of experts comprehends your needs wisely and generates scalable solutions for you with our skill in configuring the customization of Salesforce by loading and integrating legacy data using Data Loader Apex coding.

With Salesforce.com Products including Sales Cloud, Service Cloud, Force.com and Chatter, we develop various business projects by integrating them with mobile apps in order to produce profitable results. With dependable, safe, assessable and seamless incorporation of Salesforce.com with the on-premise apps such as ERP, Databases, Accounting Inventory, XML Files and others; we easily migrate your customer data from other apps to Salesforce which makes it easier for you to carry out your business workflow.

  • Integration with Accounting systems or ERP systems or Web app using PHP/ .NET
